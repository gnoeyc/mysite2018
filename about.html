Professional Bio

I am a radiologist subspecializing in musculoskeletal imaging and interventional procedures. I practice in Singapore General Hospital (SGH), Singapore. I graduated from National University of Singapore (NUS) Yong Yoo Lin School of Medicine in 2006, attained NUS Master of Medicine (Diagnostic Radiology) and United Kingdom Royal College of Radiologists Fellowship in 2012. My research interest includes sports imaging, MRI, radiology informatics and medical education.
