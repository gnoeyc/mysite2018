Who Am I?

Doctor | Radiologist

Short Bio

I am a radiologist subspecializing in musculoskeletal imaging and interventional procedures. I practice in Singapore General Hospital (SGH) a tertiary academic medical institution in Singapore. I graduated from National University of Singapore (NUS) Yong Yoo Lin School of Medicine in 2006, attained NUS Master of Medicine (Diagnostic Radiology) and United Kingdom Royal College of Radiologists Fellowship in 2012. My areas of research interest includes MRI, sports imaging and radiological informatics. I am also active in education, holding appointment of department Director of Training as well as Adjunct Assistant Profession from Duke-NUS Medical School. I am married with two boys and my hobbies are basketball, console gaming, reading and travelling.
